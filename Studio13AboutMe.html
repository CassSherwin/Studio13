  • Meet the Owner

    Carrie B.

    Don't let the purple hair & infectious laughter fool you. Carrie has been a stylist & colorist for 30 years. After working in other salons, where she garnered a loyal following, Carrie decided to branch out on her own. Studio13 is a private suite salon where she can focus on her clients' hair desires in a relaxed, fun atmosphere. No style is too conservative or too crazy. Carrie's talents as a colorist are as amazing as her cuts & styles. Her clients (men & women) can get colors that are natural or bright & bold. She is equally adept with gorgeous blondes, vibrant reds, rich browns, bold vivids, & everything in between.

    From Carrie:

    "I love the variety of my clients & my time dedicated with them. I enjoy doing all cuts from sexy long layers to razor cuts, pompadours, & tight fades...heck I can even do a fade with a razor. I want all my clients to have a great time & leave happy."
